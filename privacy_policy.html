Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Quantum Reactor ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web-based idle game application ("the Game"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the Game.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Automatically Collected

We may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device characteristics for fingerprinting (used for referral system integrity)
  • Screen resolution
  • Time zone offset
  • Language preferences
  • Gameplay statistics and progress

2.2 Local Storage Data

The Game stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Game progress and statistics
  • User preferences (language settings)
  • Referral codes and processed referrals
  • Achievement and upgrade data

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment information
  • Location data beyond time zone
  • Social media profiles or contacts

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Game functionality
  • To save and restore your game progress
  • To implement the referral system and prevent abuse
  • To personalize your gaming experience (language preferences)
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the Game

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Since this is a client-side web application, your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers, except as described below:

  • We may use GitHub Pages analytics for basic usage statistics
  • Browser-level data may be accessible to GitHub as the hosting provider
  • No personal data is shared with advertisers or third-party services

5. Data Security

Your game data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We implement the following security measures:

  • All game logic runs client-side with no server transmission of personal data
  • Device fingerprinting is used only for referral system integrity
  • No sensitive personal information is collected or stored
  • HTTPS encryption is used for all connections

6. Data Retention

Game data is stored locally on your device indefinitely until you:

  • Clear your browser's localStorage manually
  • Clear your browser data/cookies
  • Uninstall your browser
  • Use private/incognito browsing mode

7. Children's Privacy

The Game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information from any users, this policy applies equally to all users regardless of age.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Your game data is accessible to you through the game interface
  • Deletion: You can delete your data by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • Portability: Your game data is stored in a standard JSON format
  • Control: You can disable referral tracking by clearing localStorage

9. Third-Party Services

The Game is hosted on GitHub Pages. Please review GitHub's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices:

  • GitHub Privacy Statement

10. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers by our application. However, GitHub Pages may involve data processing in various countries according to their privacy policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date below. Changes become effective immediately upon posting.

12. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Game uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to store game data. This data is:

  • Stored only on your local device
  • Not transmitted to any servers
  • Used only for game functionality
  • Removable by clearing browser data

13. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can contact us through:

  • GitHub Issues: Create an issue on our repository
  • GitHub Discussions: Start a discussion

Note: Since this is an open-source project with no commercial backend, we do not provide direct email support.

14. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing is:

  • Legitimate Interest: To provide game functionality and prevent abuse
  • Consent: By using the Game, you consent to this data processing

15. Open Source Notice

This game is open source. You can review our data handling practices by examining the source code at: https://github.com/gerandios/quantum-reactor
